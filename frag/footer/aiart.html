Statement on AI Art

Synthesizer V Studio makes significant use of machine learning technologies. The machine learning dataset (samples) collected for these purposes are exclusively sourced from voice providers who have consented to this process, are paid for their work, and have signed contracts indicating as such.

The creator of this website does not condone the use of AI art generators or voice changer models created with learning materials gathered in a non-consensual manner.
