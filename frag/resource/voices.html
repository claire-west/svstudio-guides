A reference of all SVDs (singing voice databases) for Synthesizer V Studio.

Purchase links are provided below for a number of storefronts, including non-English options. Synthesizer V Studio and related voice databases are not region-specific. The software is the same regardless of where you purchase it from, and it does not require any specific locale settings on your computer.

If you are just starting out, you can download Synthesizer V Studio Basic and SOLARIA lite to try out the software for free. Please be mindful that the free options offer a reduced feature set and lower quality synthesis when compared to the Pro edition with a paid voice database.

Disclaimer regarding Taobao proxy services

Some voice databases are only available on Taobao, a Chinese web store that can be difficult for international buyers to access.

International buyers should attempt to make a Taobao account first, however account creation or payment may not be possible for some people for a variety of reasons. For these cases, links have been provided below to a proxy service called ParcelUp, which is a third party company that makes purchases from Taobao on behalf of international buyers. This involves a service charge, and orders are processed manually, often taking 1-3 days to be completed.

While many buyers of SynthV products have used ParcelUp without issue, this is not an endorsement. It is your responsibility to adequately familiarize yourself with the fees involved and available alternatives.

Synthesizer V Studio Pro

Synthesizer V Studio Pro can be purchased from Dreamtonics, AHS, or DLsite. Once you have completed your order and saved your product code in a safe place, you can download the installer from the Dreamtonics store, regardless of which website you purchased from.

A digital bundle is available from the Dreamtonics Store which saves 10% on Pro and one voice database.

A physical starter pack (AHS, Amazon Japan) is available which includes the choice of one voice database sold by AHS.

You can also get 30% off your first purchase on DLsite by using the code DLsiteWelcome30 during account creation.

Buyers in mainland China can purchase from Dreamtonics on Taobao or Quadimension on Taobao.

Free voice database for Synthesizer V Studio Pro

Purchase of Synthesizer V Studio Pro always includes the voice database Mai at no extra cost. This voice database must be installed separately, but will be automatically activated when used with the Pro edition of the software.

Mai cannot be purchased separately or used in Synthesizer V Studio Basic.
