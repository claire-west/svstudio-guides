Who is Claire?

Claire is a vocal synth tuner and aspiring producer from Canada working under the name VerA. She focuses primarily on work with Synthesizer V Studio, but endeavors to work with more engines in the future. Claire's main inspirations are CircusP, Flanger Moose, *Luna, inabakumori, and Porter Robinson, among many others.

To inquire about tuning work or collaboration, send a message on the official SynthV Forums.

Synthesizer V Studio

All AI voices for SynthV Studio have multilingual capabilities and can sing in English, Japanese, and Mandarin Chinese regardless of the native language for the voice, however a subtle accent is normal when using cross-lingual synthesis.

I can use Standard voices to sing in other languages, with varying degrees of success. Generally speaking Chinese Standard voices can sing in Japanese and English with relatively good understandability, though some words will not be enunciated very well and there will be a noticeable accent (see the examples below). Using Japanese Standard voices to sing in English has significantly more drawbacks due to a smaller set of available phonemes, and I would not recommend it.

I am not able to tune or work on Mandarin Chinese songs, as I am not suitably familiar with the language.
